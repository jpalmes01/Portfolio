Greyhounds Oasis

Greyhounds Only is a 501c(3) greyhound rescue organization based in Chicago, IL that is committed to placing retired racing and at-risk greyhounds into responsible, loving homes. By providing a more transparent and user-centric experience. The Greyhounds Only redesign hopes to foster stronger connections between adopters and greyhounds, ultimately leading to more successful adoptions and happier homes for these wonderful companions.
